> Sources: Addendum v2.1 §§A6, C · PRD v2.0 §6 · Tracker Spec §7

## Cosmetic Shop (Decision A6)

FP is spendable on **both functional stat upgrades and cosmetics** — two shops, one shared currency.

> Cosmetics are never required for progression. They're purely expressive.

### Shop Sections

| Section | Content | Refresh |
|---------|---------|---------|
| **Featured** | 3–5 items | Weekly rotation |
| **Catalog** | Organized by category | Permanent |
| **Seasonal Vault** | Prestige & event-exclusive items earned | Permanent (display only) |

### Cosmetic Categories

| Category | FP Cost Range | Examples |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| **Color Palettes** | 50–100 FP | Override stat-derived colors |
| **Particle Effects** | 100–200 FP | Trailing sparks, ambient glow, orbiting shapes |
| **Accessories** | 150–300 FP | Geometric "armor" overlays, crowns, eye mods |
| **Evolution Skins** | 500 FP | Alternate themes per evolution stage (must reach stage first) |
| **Seasonal Exclusives** | — | Earned only from prestige/tower milestones (not purchasable) |

### Price Calibration

A player earning ~150–200 FP/workout should afford **one mid-tier cosmetic every 7–10 workouts** while keeping up with stat allocation and feeding.

Most expensive items (~500 FP) represent **3–4 weeks of dedicated earning** — aspirational but achievable.

### Spending Tension (By Design)

Players who prioritize cosmetics progress **slower in the tower** but have a more personalized pet. Players investing in stats have a **stronger fighter** but more generic-looking creature. This tension is intentional.

## Achievement System

Achievements provide secondary goals across four visible categories plus hidden surprises.

### Training Milestones

| Achievement | Requirement |
|-------------|------------|
| First Blood | Complete your first workout |
| Iron Will | Log 1,000 total reps |
| Rep Machine | Log 10,000 total reps |
| PR Hunter | Hit 10 personal records |
| Volume King | Log 500 total reps in a single session |

### Consistency Badges

| Achievement | Requirement |
|-------------|------------|
| Momentum | 3-day workout streak |
| Unstoppable | 7-day streak |
| Iron Habit | 4 consecutive weeks without missing a scheduled workout |
| The Grind | 100 total workouts |

### Pet Milestones

| Achievement | Requirement |
|-------------|------------|
| First Form | Evolve to Stage 2 |
| Apex Predator | Reach Stage 4 |
| Well Fed | Feed your pet 50 times |
| Balanced Build | All stats within 20% of each other |
| Glass Cannon | Any stat more than 3x your lowest stat |

### Tower Achievements

| Achievement | Requirement |
|-------------|------------|
| Tower Rookie | Clear floor 10 |
| Silver Climber | Clear floor 25 |
| Gold Standard | Clear floor 50 |
| Platinum Legend | Clear floor 100 |
| Boss Slayer | Defeat 10 boss floors |

### Hidden Achievements

Undiscovered badges rewarding unusual behavior:
- **Early Bird** — Workout at 5am
- **Century Set** — Log 100 reps in a single set
- **Giant Killer** — Defeat a boss 20 levels above expected range
- **Dedicated Caretaker** — Feed pet immediately after every workout for 2 weeks

## Weekly Quest System

Rotating mini-challenges that add variety. Refresh every Monday.

### Quest Types

| Category | Example Quests | FP Reward |
|----------|---------------|-----------|
| **Volume** | "Log 500 total reps this week" | 25–50 FP |
| **Consistency** | "Train 4 days this week" | 25–50 FP |
| **Technique** | "Complete 3 sessions with Tempo intent" | 30–60 FP |
| **Balance** | "Do at least one Push, Pull, and Leg session" | 30–50 FP |
| **Pet** | "Feed your pet 3 times" / "Attempt 2 tower floors" | 20–40 FP |

### Weekly Structure
- **3 quests** per week (easy, medium, hard)
- Completing all three → **+25 FP bonus**
- Generated based on player's active program and history (always achievable)

> Quests never require training more than the player's program prescribes. A 3-day program player won't get a quest requiring 5 days.
